Our History

Founded in 2020, Magmon Shop was born out of a passion for health and wellness. The name “Magmon” is a tribute to the founders’ parents, combining the names Magloire and Monique. Our journey began with a simple goal: to make innovative and high-quality wellness products accessible to everyone.

Since then, we’ve grown into a trusted name, known for our dedication to improving the quality of life of our customers.

Today, we continue to expand our range of products, always focused on bringing cutting-edge solutions that enhance well-being and convenience.

MagMon Shop-Company Timeline

  1. 2020 – Foundation • Magmon Shop is founded by Kabuya Tambwe Josué in Cape Town, inspired by a passion for health and wellness. • The name Magmon honors his parents, Magloire and Monique, reflecting family values and dedication.
  2. 2021 – First Product Line Launch • Introduction of the first range of innovative wellness and lifestyle products. • Early partnerships with local suppliers help establish a strong quality standard.
  3. 2022 – Expansion & Recognition • Magmon Shop opens nationwide delivery across South Africa. • Receives positive reviews for high-quality products and customer care.
  4. 2023 – Leadership Team Grows • Nathan Kabange joins as Director of Marketing & Wellness, bringing fresh branding and outreach strategies. • Genovic Kabulo becomes Technical Director, enhancing e-commerce systems and digital presence.
  5. 2024 – Community & Sustainability • Launch of eco-friendly packaging and a wellness-education campaign. • Builds a loyal customer community through workshops and online events.
  6. 2025 – Looking Ahead • Preparing to introduce a premium product line and explore international shipping. • Continuing to innovate while staying true to the mission of improving everyday well-being.
