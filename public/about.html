About Me

Hello! I’m Kabuya Tambwe Josué, an ambitious student originally from the Congo, now pursuing my studies in South Africa. I completed my high school diploma in 2022 at Complexe Solaire Galaxy, majoring in science.

After my studies, I gained valuable experience working for six months in mining and metallurgical operations at Somika. This experience broadened my perspective and fueled my passion for innovative solutions.

In search of greater opportunities, I relocated to South Africa and am currently pursuing a diploma in Information Technology in Software Development at Rosebank College.

Along the way, I’ve expanded my knowledge by completing courses such as CS50 from Harvard through edX and a Full-Stack Web Development course on Udemy, as well as a Java Android project course.

My journey into software development stems from a deep desire to make a difference. I’m passionate about technology and engineering because I believe they are the keys to solving real-world problems and shaping the future.

I’ve also actively participated in tech conferences and events like Google Hackathon and DevFest, helping me build a strong network and stay current with industry trends.

I’m excited about the journey ahead and eager to continue learning and making a positive impact in the world of technology.
